Listing Intelligence

Every listing scored. Every signal ranked.

ShowIntel scores every active listing in your inventory across three signal buckets — Market Pressure, Showing Intelligence, and Seller Relationship Health. Each bucket carries a configurable weight so the algorithm reflects your market, your team size, and where your business is right now. The output is not a dashboard of charts. It is a ranked list of listings that need attention today, each paired with one plain-English recommended action. No manual review. No guesswork. No Monday morning report-pulling. The listings that need your attention surface themselves.

AskIntel

Ask your inventory anything.

AskIntel is the AI intelligence layer built into ShowIntel. Ask any question about your active inventory, your showing activity, or your pricing history in plain English — and receive an answer grounded in your team's own proprietary data. Not national averages. Not market generalizations. Your data, your market, your patterns. AskIntel maintains session memory so follow-up questions work naturally. The longer your team runs ShowIntel, the more precise AskIntel's answers become. This is not a chatbot. It is an intelligence briefing engine built for listing professionals.

Scheduling

One place to book, assign, and sync. Nothing falls through.

From confirmation to calendar, ShowIntel handles the full showing workflow in one place. Your coordinator or Director of Sales confirms the appointment, assigns any agent on your team, and pushes everything to your CRM and calendar without leaving the platform. Showing agents, buyer tours, open houses — any appointment type, any agent, fully tracked and synced. No duplicate entry. No chasing confirmations across text threads and calendar apps. Every appointment lives in one place, visible to everyone who needs it, synced everywhere it belongs.

Seller Portal

Transparency that strengthens the relationship.

Every seller on your team gets a branded, secure portal tied to their listing — your logo, your colors, your team name. ShowIntel is completely invisible. Sellers see showing activity, key stats, and closing documents on demand without a phone call. Your admin controls exactly what surfaces and when. Your agents receive the AI-powered recommendations on the backend to make informed decisions before every seller conversation. The portal does not replace the relationship. It removes the noise so every touchpoint is intentional. Informed sellers are confident sellers. Confident sellers close.
