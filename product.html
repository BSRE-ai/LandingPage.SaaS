Listing Intelligence

Every listing scored. Every signal ranked.

ShowIntel scores every active listing in your inventory across three signal buckets: market pressure, showing velocity, and seller relationship health. The output is not a chart. It is a ranked list of listings that need attention today, each with one plain-English recommended action. Algorithm weights are fully configurable so your scoring reflects your market, your team size, and where your business is right now.

AI Insights

Ask your inventory anything.

ShowIntel's AI layer answers natural language questions from your team's own proprietary data: not generic market averages, not county-level statistics. Which listing is most at risk. How your pricing accuracy trended over the last six closings. The answers come from your history, your market, and your team's performance patterns. The longer you run it, the more precise it gets.

Scheduling

One place to book, assign, and sync. Nothing falls through.

Book, assign, and sync in one motion. Your coordinator or Director of Sales confirms the appointment, assigns any agent on your team, and pushes everything to your CRM and calendar without leaving the platform. Showing agents, buyer tours, open houses: any appointment type, any agent, fully tracked. No duplicate entry. No chasing down who is showing what and when. Everything in one place, visible to everyone who needs to see it.

Seller Portal

Transparency that strengthens the relationship.

Every seller on your team gets a branded, secure portal tied to their listing. Showing activity, feedback, and closing documents: available on demand, without the phone call. Your admin controls exactly what sellers see. Your agents get the AI-powered recommendations on the backend to make informed decisions before every seller conversation. The portal does not replace the relationship. It makes every touchpoint more intentional.
