Your listings are talking. Are you listening?

Built For

The person accountable for every listing's outcome.

ShowIntel is built for the Team Leader or Director of Sales at a listing-heavy team carrying 10 to 50 active listings. Not for coordinators. Not for buyer-side teams. Not for single-agent operations running light inventory. For the person who walks into the listing appointment, has the price conversation with the seller, decides when to recommend a reduction, and carries accountability for every listing's outcome. If you spend part of every week reviewing listings one by one — pulling feedback, checking DOM, fielding seller calls — and wish that process were faster, smarter, and more proactive, ShowIntel was built for you.

What It Is Not

ShowIntel does not replace your CRM, your calendar, or your workflow. It makes all of them smarter.

ShowIntel is not a CRM. It does not manage contacts, run email campaigns, send automated follow-ups, or replace the tools your team already depends on. It does not require you to change how you work. It sits on top of your existing stack — your CRM, your calendar, your showing service — and fills the gap no CRM was designed to fill: real-time listing health, seller confidence monitoring, showing intelligence, and AI-powered recommendations at the listing level. Your team keeps the tools they know. ShowIntel makes all of them smarter.

Your Data. Your Edge.

The longer you run it, the smarter it gets.

Every closing, every showing, every pricing outcome your team records in ShowIntel becomes part of your proprietary dataset. Over time, the scoring algorithm becomes calibrated to your specific market, your price bands, and your team's historical performance patterns. The Pre-Listing Signal Tool unlocks as your closed data grows — at 50 records it becomes active, and at 100 records it becomes predictive. No other team's data influences your scores or your answers. Your history is your competitive advantage. No competitor can replicate 18 months of your own closed listing data. The algorithm is not the moat. Your data is.
