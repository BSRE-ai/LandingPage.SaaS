Your listings are talking. Are you listening?

Built For

The person accountable for every listing's outcome.

ShowIntel is built for the Team Leader or Director of Sales at a listing-heavy team carrying 10 to 50 active listings. Not for coordinators. Not for buyer-side teams. For the person who walks into the listing appointment, has the price conversation with the seller, and decides when to recommend a reduction. If you review your active inventory every week and wish the process were faster and smarter, ShowIntel was built for you.

What It Is Not

ShowIntel does not replace your CRM, your calendar, or your workflow. It makes all of them smarter.

ShowIntel is not a CRM. It does not manage contacts, run email campaigns, or replace the tools your team already depends on. It is the intelligence layer that sits on top of your existing stack and fills the gap no CRM was designed to fill: real-time listing health, seller confidence monitoring, and AI-powered recommendations at the listing level.

Your Data. Your Edge.

The longer you run it, the smarter it gets.

Every closing, every showing, every outcome gets added to your team's proprietary dataset. Over time, ShowIntel's algorithm becomes calibrated to your market, your price bands, and your team's performance patterns. No competitor can replicate 18 months of your own closed listing history. The algorithm is not the moat. Your data is.
