What It Knows

Role-aware. Data-grounded. Never guessing.

AskIntel is not a general-purpose AI assistant. It operates exclusively within your team's ShowIntel data — active listing health, showing activity, seller relationship signals, pricing trends, and market comparisons. Every response is injected with your team's live data at query time. The AI never answers from general real estate knowledge when your data is available.

Access is role-aware. A showing agent sees showing-relevant answers. An agent sees their listings. A Team Leader sees the full portfolio. Role enforcement happens in the backend before any data reaches the AI — not in the prompt.

The Compounding Advantage

A competitor can copy the interface. They cannot copy your history.

AskIntel gets smarter the longer your team runs ShowIntel. Every closing, every showing, every pricing outcome adds to your team's proprietary dataset. After 12 to 18 months, AskIntel's answers are calibrated to your specific market, your price bands, and your team's performance patterns.

No other team's data influences your answers. Ever. Your data is isolated, scoped, and owned entirely by your team's instance of ShowIntel.

"ShowIntel shows the data. AskIntel tells you what it means."

What It Is Not

AskIntel is not a general-purpose chatbot. It does not answer questions outside real estate and your ShowIntel data. It is not a decision-maker — it surfaces recommendations and the team decides. It does not write to your CRM. It does not communicate with sellers. It does not replace the Team Leader's judgment.

It surfaces what the data says. The call is always yours.
